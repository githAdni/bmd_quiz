# Privacy Policy for BMD Quiz

**Last updated: December 24, 2024**

## Introduction

Adni Quiz operates the BMD Quiz mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of this App.**

The BMD Quiz app is designed to work completely offline and does not:
- Collect personal information
- Track user behavior
- Use analytics services
- Require user accounts or registration
- Access your contacts, location, or any other personal data
- Share any data with third parties

## Data Storage

All quiz progress and scores are stored locally on your device only. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
